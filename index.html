What exactly is
Morning Buddy?
Morning Buddy is an AI-powered app that gives you a personalised morning call with a friendly chat, so you wake up happier and ready to tackle the day.
How do the
“calls” work?
You pick the days and times you want us to wake you up (with different voices and personalities). On those mornings you’ll receive a literal morning call from your buddy instead of a harsh alarm.
What will the
calls cost me?
Free

Morning Buddy calls are completely free during early access. Later you can choose to join  Morning Buddy + for longer calls, more buddies and extra insights – but you never have to pay a penny if you don’t want to.
